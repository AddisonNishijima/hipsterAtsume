Hipster Atsume

Instructions

Combine items to try to entice hipsters to appear!

How to add items to your inventory:

  • Buy an item in the store by clicking on it and the item will appear in your inventory!

    • (WARNING: This will use monies!!!)

  • Click it in the store again to remove it from your inventory

    • (Note: You cannot sell back items for monies!! (cause Capitalism)

  • You can only have 4 inventory items, so choose carefully!

    • (Note: You can buy up to 8 with monies!)

How to add items to the 'yard':

  • Click an item in the inventory and it will appear in the yard!
  • Click it in the inventory again to remove it from the yard
  • If you get the right combo, a hipster will appear!

You need at least 2 items in your inventory to attract a hipster!

Try to collect all the hipsters!!!
